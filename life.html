biography

Blending my passion for web development and expertise in SEO to provide freelancing services to businesses internationally.

I have spent my entire adult life creating systems. I consider well-organized, complex structures a thing of pure beauty. "Work smart" is not solely a professional philosophy— this mantra embodies my approach to life—and is realized in the combination of my passion for building and problem solving to address issues of inefficiency, resource waste, and corporate stagnation. I am very comfortable with technical concepts of varying complexities.

I spent the past year at a premier Coalition Technologies a premier digital marketing firm located in Culver City. While there, I focused on honing my skills in analytical analysis, planning and implementing SEO campaigns, and understanding the intricacies of setting overall campaign strategy. Having learned SEO via this immersive agency experience, I'm comfortable speaking to implementation and optimization issues across a broad range of industries.

My initial foray into the tech world began at Powered Office, where I managed a portfolio of twenty accounts, ushering them through our development process. Prior to my promotion, I managed a team of seven where I restructured internal processes, and implemented new processes, systems & tools to augment employee productivity by 45%, reduce resource waste and minimize corporate liability.
